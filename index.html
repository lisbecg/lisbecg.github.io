    Mission Statement

    Our mission is to help Hispanic people to succeed in college by connecting with other members of the Hispanic community.

    Let us succeed together!

    • Immigrants accounted for 13.7% of the United States population in 2020. Even though the population of Hispanic people continues growing, the retention and graduation rates across all levels of education among Hispanics are low when compared to their non-Hispanic counterparts. For example, only 1 in 10 Hispanic students who start elementary school graduate from college.
    • Several factors are causing Hispanics in the United Statesâ€™ educational system to drop out and not continue their studies. Not being proficient in English, lack of information about financial aid, lack of guide to navigate American culture, and computer tools that are not localized in the native language are some of the challenges that Hispanic people face.
    • We believe in the power that the Hispanic community has. We aim to connect Hispanics that want to advance their education to other Hispanics that can advise and help them to do so.
